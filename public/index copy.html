The Dewy Sun kiss Foundation

PSAUME 4 was created as a solution for self-love. PSAUME 4 has all the ingredients to naturally enhance the beauty of women of colour. It focuses on your skin textures and problems in terms of skincare and makeup as never a foundation has done before.

How to Apply: Using ﬁngers, apply the formula to face working from the inside outward. Build coverage as needed and let the sun kiss you!

Composition: When pressed, this liquid sun kiss foundation lasts in even the most environmentally strenuous conditions and provides glow and freshness. Enriched with Honey, milk, rose water and hyaluronic acid, the formula illuminates the complexion, tingled the skin to diminish wrinkles for the look of luminous natural skin.

Product: The dewy sun kiss foundation is a full coverage foundation provides 16-hour wear with a natural glow ﬁnish. The lightweight foundation glides onto skin for superb comfort and a perfect-looking complexion, infused with a heavenly almond-coconut smell.
