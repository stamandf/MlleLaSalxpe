PSALM FOUR Organic Tinted Face Cream

Protective cream for all skin types

PSALM FOUR was created specifically for People of color as a solution for self-love. It is a day cream specially formulated to protect the skin of the face, neck and décolleté area from photo-aging.

Composed of mineral ﬁlters and antioxidant organic ingredients, PSALM FOUR provides a daily protection against the abuse of elements (sun, pollution) while concealing the imperfections. Its formula is rich in soothing and emollient ingredients. Luxuriant in polysaccharides, vitamins and minerals, it preserves the skin's moisture and improves its overall texture to provide a visibly brighter and more even complexion.

Applied every morning, PSALM FOUR cream wraps the skin in a soft and silky protective veil that lasts throughout the day.

Benefits includes reduced imperfections, a more uniform complexion and hydrated skin, soft to the touch.

How to Apply: Use your hands to apply the cream on your face and neck. Apply daily to perfectly cleansed skin. For external use only. Avoid contact with the eye area.

Key ingredients: Aloe Vera Gel, Zinc Oxide, Caprylic Triglyceride, Propenediol, Titanium Dioxide, Glyceryl Stearate, Salicylic Acid, Glycerin, Tocopherol, Jojoba Esters.

Gluten Free, non GMO, not tested on animals
